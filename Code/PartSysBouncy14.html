Particle System Controls:
r/R key: refresh/Reset. Space-bar: single-step. p or P key: pause/resume.
c or C key: toggle clear-screen. f or F: toggle 'fountain' (age constraint)
d/D key: less/more drag. g/G key: less/more gravity
s or S: switch solvers (implicit vs. explicit)
b or B: floor-bounce method

Buttons adjust 'r' or 'R' key strength
